Chamber of Commerce Website Plan

Site Name

Billings Chamber of Commerce

I chose to create a website for the Chamber of Commerce of Billings, Montana, USA. I grew up in Billings and just recently moved back to the city. I have spent a lot of time in community involvement and leadership in this town, and am familiar with both old and new businesses in the area.

Site Purpose

As the largest city in the state of Montana, Billings is key to economic growth in the Western Montana region. The Billings Chamber of Commerce will provide a place for local businesses and other community members and organizations to communicate together and participate in community leadership and policy.

Scenarios

What new businesses will be opening in this area in the coming year?

Where can I find the contact information of local business and community leaders?

What kind of information or incentives does the Billings Chamber of Commerce provide to prospective businesses?
