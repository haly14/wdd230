Membership Level Benefits

NP Membership (Non-Profit)

Cost: No Annual Fee

Non-profit organizations may join the Chamber of Commerce at no cost to them. Thank you for the valuable contributions you make to our community.

Bronze Membership

Cost: $250 (Annual)

Bronze Membership allows businesses and organizations to attend our annual training and meet-and-greet. Bronze members receive our basic advertisement package and 10% discounts for participation in city events.

Silver Membership

Cost: $350 (Annual)

Silver Membership allows businesses and organizations to attend quarterly training and meet-and-greets. Silver members receive our standard advertisement package and 20% discounts for participation in city events.

Gold Membership

Cost: $500 (Annual)

Gold Membership allows businesses and organizations to attend monthly training and meet-and-greets. Gold members receive our premium advertisement package and 35% discounts for participation in city events. Gold Membership businesses also have the ability to be nominated as a spotlight on our website homepage.
